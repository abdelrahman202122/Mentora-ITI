# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mentora serves learners, tutors, and internal platform operators. Learners use it to find trustworthy tutors, compare fit, book sessions, pay, and continue conversations. Tutors use it to manage their teaching profile, subjects, bookings, earnings, and learner communication. Admin users need clear operational surfaces for monitoring and managing the marketplace.

## Product Purpose

Mentora is a private education marketplace that connects learners with tutors through tutor discovery, AI-powered matching, booking management, real-time communication, payments, and reviews. Success means learners can confidently find and book the right tutor, tutors can manage their work without friction, and the platform feels reliable across the full learning journey.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, modern, friendly. The interface should feel capable and supportive, with enough warmth to make education feel personal and enough restraint to make important actions feel safe.

## Anti-references

Mentora should not feel like a generic AI SaaS product. Avoid vague automation-heavy copy, decorative AI gradients, over-polished template sections, and visual choices that make the product feel more like a demo than a learning marketplace.

## Design Principles

- Make trust visible through clear states, predictable actions, and honest feedback.
- Keep learning goals and tutor fit at the center of every discovery and AI flow.
- Use familiar product patterns so learners and tutors can focus on their task.
- Let friendliness come from language, spacing, and supportive empty states rather than decoration.
- Preserve clarity across English and Arabic localization, including future RTL surfaces.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA for product surfaces. Maintain readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, semantic form errors, reduced-motion alternatives, and localization-aware layouts for English and Arabic users.
